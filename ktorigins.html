Where the Horror Began!


K'tah! Origins (Project Name: K'torigins) takes us back to the beginning of the outbreak... before our heroes met, before the giant boulder became sentient, and before a 3rd dimension was added to reality. In K'torigins, you play as one of our isolated heroes scrambling to survive the onslought of the zombie horde... in 2D!


Prepare Your Adventure


Configure your game settings below. When you are ready, click the K'torigins! button at the bottom.


Choose a Character: your character will have a special ability to fend off the hordes.


Make a custom maze: your maze will generated based off the following layout. (When deleting something, be sure to replace the deleted element).

'P': player, 'X': walls, '.': spaces, 'Z': zombies

The first and last rows and columns must all be walls. There must be the same number of rows and columns. There must be only one player, at least one zombie, and no other characters than the ones specified above.

Need some inspiration? Try out one of these and paste them above!


Choose a Difficulty: higher difficulty means more zombies that bite harder and faster!
